Everything You Need

Multiple AI Models

Access Gemini, GPT-Image, and more. Switch between models instantly to find the perfect style for your vision.

Bring Your Own Keys

Use your own API keys stored securely in your Mac's Keychain. Currently powered by Replicate to access all models. No middleman, no hidden costs.

Reference Images

Guide AI generation with reference images. Sketch directly on them to show exactly what you want.

Background Removal

One-click background removal powered by AI. Perfect for product shots and compositing.

Project Organization

Keep your generations organized with projects. Full metadata, activity history, and easy library management.

Privacy First

Everything runs locally on your Mac. Your images and API keys never touch external servers beyond the AI providers.
